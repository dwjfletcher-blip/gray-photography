Offerings

Services

Gray Photography offers a focused set of services built around honest, monochrome work — digital, film, and medium format.

01

Portrait Sessions

Digital Film Indoor / Outdoor

Candid and posed portrait sessions shot in a natural, relaxed style. Available on digital for fast turnaround or on film for a timeless quality. No forced smiles — just honest moments.

Includes

  • 1–2 hour session
  • Location scouting
  • Digital delivery of edited selects
  • Film option: scanned high-res files
02

Prints

All Formats Fine Art Various Sizes

Fine art prints made from digital, 35mm film, or medium format negatives. Printed on archival matte paper. Available in a range of sizes — from desk-friendly 5×7 to large-format 16×20.

Includes

  • Archival paper stock
  • Signed & numbered editions
  • Sizes: 5×7, 8×10, 11×14, 16×20
  • Custom sizing available
03

Film Scanning

35mm 120 / Medium Format High Resolution

High-resolution scanning of developed 35mm and 120 medium format film. Delivered as lossless TIFF files with basic dust removal. Bring your negatives to life in digital form without losing the grain and character of film.

Includes

  • 35mm & 120 format rolls
  • High-res TIFF delivery
  • Basic dust & scratch removal
  • Private download link
04

Event Photography

Digital Candid Small Events

Unobtrusive coverage of small events — gatherings, shows, studio sessions, or everyday occasions worth remembering. Shot in a documentary style: no posing, no staging, just the room as it is.

Includes

  • Up to 4 hours of coverage
  • Edited selects delivered digitally
  • Monochrome edit as standard
  • Online gallery for sharing

How It Works

The Process

01
Get in Touch

Reach out with your project idea. Sessions, prints, scanning — describe what you're looking for and we'll go from there.

02
Plan Together

We'll align on format (digital, film, or medium format), location, timing, and any specific shots or moods you have in mind.

03
Shoot & Develop

The session or project is completed. Film is sent for development and scanning; digital files are culled and edited by hand.

04
Delivery

Edited files are delivered via a private online gallery or as physical prints, depending on what was arranged.
